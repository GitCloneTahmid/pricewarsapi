2. Privacy Policy

We collect minimal usage data required for API billing and rate limiting. We do not sell, trade, or transfer your proprietary pricing data to third parties. Your SKU lists, cost inputs, and margin thresholds are treated as highly confidential trade secrets. Data is stored on AWS servers located in the US-East region unless a specific residency is requested via Enterprise contract.

3. Data Usage & Neutrality

PriceWars.api acts as a neutral infrastructure provider. We do not aggregate client data to create "market averages" or shared insights. Your data is siloed logically and physically from other tenants. We utilize a read-only access pattern for competitor websites to ensure compliance with their robots.txt where applicable, or utilize licensed data feeds where scraping is restricted.

4. Compliance & Risk

Our algorithms are deterministic and audit-logged. In the event of a pricing error or "race to the bottom" scenario, our system includes a mandatory "Circuit Breaker" feature that halts updates if price variance exceeds defined safety parameters (default 15%). You maintain full legal liability for the final prices displayed to your consumers.
