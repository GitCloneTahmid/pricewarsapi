About PriceWars.api

Founded in 2021 by a team of high-frequency trading engineers and retail economists, PriceWars.api was built to solve a single problem: the information asymmetry in global e-commerce.

Our Mission

To provide businesses with the same level of algorithmic sophistication used by top-tier financial institutions. We believe that margin protection should be automated, deterministic, and accessible via a single API call.

The Technology

Our infrastructure spans 14 global regions, utilizing a proprietary mesh of residential proxy networks to ensure 100% data accuracy without the risk of competitor spoofing or IP blocking.

Transparency First

Unlike "black box" AI solutions, PriceWars provides full logic logs. You will always know why a price was recommended and which guardrails were triggered.
